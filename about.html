About Me

A good user experience is the result of proper execution of both design and code.

Which is why I do both.

I am an innovative UI/UX designer and front end web developer with a passion for details.

My Story

I began my career with a degree in digital media, giving me a classically trained eye for good design. Armed with a wide array of skills, I’ve experienced design from all angles of the field, from video and photo editing to print materials to pitch decks to web page design. Working at SideReel, I strengthened my front end developer skills, creating mockups and then coding pages for the content team, while also developing graphics for the site. After 8 years of generalized design experience, I decided to focus in on user interface design, and now have a certificate in UI design.

I believe in working smart, and love coming up with streamlined solutions for processes. My experience with front end means I can easily communicate for developers, and I design around the constraints of coding. I have a demonstrated talent for creating beautiful designs. I like to take the initiative, pick up new skills, and take on new challenges. I am an extremely fast learner, familiar with Agile workflow, and approach design with a high degree of empathy for both the user and the developer.
